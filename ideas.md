# Design Brainstorm — Justice Litigation Solutions

## Brief
Boutique legal document preparation business. Elegant, feminine, professional. Deep navy + gold + white palette. Boutique legal firm feel — NOT corporate, NOT tech, NOT casual.

## Idea A — "Gilded Parchment"

**Design Movement:** Art Deco Revival meets Boutique Law

**Core Principles:**
1. Structured elegance — every element has weight and purpose
2. Warm gold accents on deep navy create a sense of authority and trust
3. Generous whitespace punctuated by gold rule lines
4. Serif-forward typography with restrained sans-serif for body

**Color Philosophy:**
- Deep navy (#0D1B3E) as the dominant authority color — evokes trust, depth, and professionalism
- Warm gold (#C9A84C) as the prestige accent — used sparingly on borders, icons, and CTAs
- Ivory/cream (#FAF7F2) as the page background — warmer than white, more boutique
- Soft slate (#6B7A99) for secondary text

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric two-column hero: large serif headline left, Lady Justice illustration right
- Horizontal gold rule dividers between sections
- Cards with subtle left-border gold accent instead of full border
- Navigation: centered logo, horizontal nav links in small-caps

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thin gold horizontal rule lines as section dividers
2. Lady Justice silhouette motif in hero and footer
3. Small-caps navigation and section labels

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states: gold underline slides in from left on nav links
- CTA buttons: gold border with navy fill, subtle shimmer on hover
- Form fields: bottom-border only (no box), gold on focus

**Animation:**
- Entrance: sections fade-up on scroll (subtle, 0.4s ease-out)
- No parallax or heavy motion — dignity over spectacle

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (serif) — headlines and section titles
- Body: Source Sans 3 (sans-serif) — clean, readable body text
- Accent: Playfair Display Italic for taglines and quotes

## Idea B — "Velvet Counsel"

**Design Movement:** Contemporary Feminine Minimalism

**Core Principles:**
1. Negative space as luxury — let content breathe
2. Deep navy as foundation, gold as punctuation
3. Soft cream sections alternate with navy sections for rhythm
4. Every page guides eye toward the intake CTA

**Color Philosophy:**
- Navy #1A2744 — deep, confident, trustworthy
- Gold #B8922A — warm, prestigious, feminine
- Cream #FDF8F0 — soft warmth, not clinical white
- Blush #F5EDE8 — ultra-subtle section tint for variety

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-width alternating sections (navy / cream / navy)
- Left-aligned text blocks with right-side decorative gold vertical bar
- Services in a 2×2 grid with icon + title + description
- Sticky header with transparent-to-navy scroll behavior

**Signature Elements:**
1. Vertical gold bar accent on left of hero text block
2. Circular Lady Justice medallion in hero
3. Gold dot separators in navigation

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Smooth scroll between anchor sections
- Button hover: gold fill slides up from bottom
- Card hover: subtle lift with gold border reveal

**Animation:**
- Staggered card entrance on scroll
- Hero text slides in from left, image fades in from right

**Typography System:**
- Display: Cormorant Garamond — ultra-elegant, feminine serif
- Body: DM Sans — modern, clean, highly readable
- Labels: DM Sans Medium in letter-spaced uppercase

## Idea C — "Ivory & Scales"

**Design Movement:** Refined Editorial

**Core Principles:**
1. Editorial rhythm — sections feel like pages of a premium brochure
2. Gold used only as line weight and icon fill — never as background
3. Navy reserved for hero and footer — cream dominates mid-sections
4. Typography does the heavy lifting

**Color Philosophy:**
- Ivory #FAFAF5 — primary background, warm and premium
- Navy #0F1E45 — hero, footer, and key CTAs
- Gold #C4922A — rule lines, icon strokes, button borders
- Charcoal #2C3E50 — body text for readability

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Magazine-style: wide left column text + narrow right column pull-quote or icon
- Full-bleed navy hero with centered serif headline
- Services as horizontal rows (not cards) with number prefix in gold
- Pricing as a clean comparison table

**Signature Elements:**
1. Numbered service rows with gold numeral prefix
2. Pull-quotes in Cormorant Garamond Italic
3. Thin gold horizontal rules as typographic ornaments

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Minimal animation — content-first
- Hover: gold underline on links, opacity shift on buttons
- Form: elegant floating labels

**Animation:**
- Fade-in only — no movement, just opacity
- Respect reduced-motion preferences

**Typography System:**
- Display: Cormorant Garamond Bold
- Body: Lato Regular
- Captions/Labels: Lato Bold uppercase letter-spaced

## Selected Approach: **Idea B — "Velvet Counsel"**

Chosen for its strong conversion focus (alternating navy/cream sections naturally guide the eye), the Cormorant Garamond + DM Sans pairing delivers the feminine-yet-authoritative feel requested, and the vertical gold bar motif creates a distinctive brand signature without being flashy.
